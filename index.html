About NMC Liberia

Serving Liberia's Muslim community with dedication since 1971

The National Muslim Council of Liberia (NMC) is the foremost Islamic organization representing the Muslim community across all fifteen counties of Liberia. Founded in 1971, the Council coordinates Islamic affairs, promotes education, and advocates for the rights and welfare of Muslims in Liberia.

Through its departments and county offices, the NMC facilitates religious programs, educational initiatives, social welfare, and interfaith dialogue — fostering peace, unity, and national development rooted in Islamic principles.

To be the unified voice of Islam in Liberia — a spiritually vibrant, intellectually empowered, and socially impactful Muslim community contributing to national peace and development.

To promote the teachings of Islam, protect the rights of Muslims, coordinate religious activities, foster Islamic education, advance social welfare, and strengthen interfaith harmony throughout Liberia.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of NMC? +
You can register as a member by filling out the online registration form in the Membership section, or visiting our headquarters at NMC Liberia, Broad Street, Monrovia.
How are prayer times calculated? +
Prayer times on this website are calculated using the Muslim World League method for Monrovia, Liberia (coordinates: 6.3°N, 10.8°W). They are recalculated daily for accuracy.
How can I donate to NMC Liberia? +
You can donate via the Donations section on this website using mobile money or card payment. You can also donate in person at any NMC office. All donations are transparent and accounted for.
How do I register for Hajj through NMC? +
Hajj registration opens annually, typically six months before the Hajj season. Visit our headquarters or contact us via email for the current registration requirements and fees.
Where is NMC Liberia headquartered? +
The NMC Liberia headquarters is located on Broad Street, Monrovia, Liberia. We have county offices in all 15 counties of Liberia.
