Welcome Message from the Chairman

National Muslim Council of Liberia

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

It is with great honor and a deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to the official website of the National Muslim Council of Liberia. This platform serves as a gateway to our mission, values, and ongoing efforts to promote unity, peace, and sustainable development within our communities and across the nation.

The National Muslim Council of Liberia stands as a unifying body committed to fostering harmony among Muslims and strengthening collaboration with our fellow citizens of all faiths. We are dedicated to advancing education, supporting humanitarian initiatives, promoting social justice, and upholding the principles of Islam that inspire compassion, integrity, and service to humanity.

Through this website, you will discover our programs, initiatives, and engagements aimed at empowering communities, nurturing youth, and contributing meaningfully to national development. We encourage you to explore, connect, and partner with us as we work together to build a more peaceful, inclusive, and prosperous Liberia.

I extend my sincere appreciation for your interest in our work and your continued support. May this platform serve as a source of information, inspiration, and collaboration for all.

Thank you, and may Allah bless you abundantly.

Alhaji Abraham Fumbah Sherif
Chairman
National Muslim Council of Liberia
